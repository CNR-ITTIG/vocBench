Users Module

The Managing Users Module is accessible to administrators, publishers, and validators. Other users do not have access to this module.

The following describes the privileges for each of these users:

  • Administrators are allowed to manage all kind of users.

  • Publishers are allowed to manage term editors, ontology editors, and validators.

  • Validators are allowed to manage term editors and ontology editors.

  • Non logged-in users can register through the link on the log-in screen. Their status and group will then be determined by a validator or administrator.


To use this module, click on the user management icon at the top of every page (visible to logged in administrators, publishers and validators).
